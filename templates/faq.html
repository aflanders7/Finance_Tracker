Frequent Questions and Answers

How do I record an expense?

Navigate to the Expenses Tracker page. Then, use the "Add An Expense" form to record your expense.

Can I delete an expense?

Use the delete button within the table to delete an expense. All deletions are permanent.

What areas of the expense form are required?

The description is the only optional input. All other fields are required to record an expense.

Why is nothing coming up when I search?

Check your spelling. If the item you are looking for still does not appear, try searching by category for the item.

What does the spending analysis show me?

The spending analysis page shows how much money you have spent per month. This is a good way to analyze your spending habits.
